Choosing the Right PSU

A stable PSU keeps your PC running efficiently and protects your components. Apex Tech leverages expert reviews to help you select the perfect PSU for your setup.

About This Tier List

Created by Sir ProvidenceLaw (SPL) and other PSU experts, this list ranks models based on efficiency, performance, and build quality. Tier A+ indicates the top performers, while other tiers provide reliable options for all budgets.

Quick Tier Guidelines

  • Budget Builds (<$750): Tier C, B, or entry-level A
  • Mid-Range Builds ($750–$1500): Tier B or standard A
  • High-End Builds (>$1500): Tier A+ for peak performance

How to Use This List

Start with the "Main List" tab to see brand → model → wattage → tier. Check the "Tiering Criteria" tab for detailed explanations. Always match PSU specs to your system's power needs and desired efficiency.

Additional Considerations

  • Wattage: Ensure your PSU handles GPU, CPU, and future upgrades.
  • Form Factor: ATX or SFX – pick what fits your case.
  • Connectors: Some GPUs require 12VHPWR (RTX 4000 series).
  • Modularity: Fully modular, semi-modular, or fixed for optimal cable management.
  • Efficiency: Look for 80+ ratings to reduce heat and save power.
